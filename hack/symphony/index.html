About

Symphony being the trendsetter in the air-cooler market and a consumer-centric brand, with its first-ever innovation challenge, wishes to engage with young developers and innovators in India.

Since inception, Symphony air coolers have been an inspiration and a symbol of futuristic and original design. This is probably why Symphony is the most recognised and copied air cooler brand in the world. At present, the organised market is flooded with at least 25 brands. If we talk about the entire universe of branded air coolers in India, the basic design and technology used are more or less the same, with just a handful of outliers. The competition is getting stiff and the market overall is heading towards saturation, in terms of innovation. In such a scenario, it becomes crucial to differentiate a brand from the competition and become the most preferred brand when consumers think of air coolers. Symphony is looking for some disruptive product ideas on two fronts: Design and Technology. It could be something never heard of, or could even be something very common but never thought of. The only thing to bear in mind is that it shouldn’t just be something good to have. Rather, it needs to be something that addresses some consumer tension at a reasonable cost. If the cost of features becomes too high, then the purpose of being competitive will be lost.

Problem Statement in detail

Product Innovation

Symphony delivers market-leading products that are a wonderful combination of design innovation, energy efficiency, distinctive styling, and customer-centricity. As a pioneer in the air-cooling industry, Symphony has continually been at the forefront of innovation. From introducing the first-ever plastic body air cooler to equipping them with world-class features viz. cool flow dispenser, i-Pure technology, touch panel, pop-up control panel, easy-fill, just to name a few, Symphony has been a trendsetter in the market.

Keeping in line with the trend of innovation and its vision to bring disrupting technology into air coolers Symphony is looking forward to having the best of design solutions that are not only too good to be true but also feasible technologically. The teams will be required to come up with prototypes in the later stage of the hackathon once their designs are approved.

Frequently Asked Question

Who can participate in Symphony Coolathon?

Symphony Coolathon is open to the Students, technologist, freelancers and professional developer community.

What is the cost of participating in Symphony Coolathon?

There is no participation fee.

How do I know my registration is confirmed and when to start the development phase?

The organizing team will approve and confirm your participation. Shortlisted teams will receive a confirmation email and it will reflect on user dashboard.

Do we need to have the entire idea fully working?

The entry should be functional for review by the judges.

As it is an online hackathon, is it acceptable to show only the prototype?

Yes, you are required to submit a prototype. If you use any database like SQL Server or PostgreSQL you can also submit a database dump along with your submission.

If it is a team submission, does that mean all team members will have access to work at the same time?

Yes, all team members can login from their account and submit application.
